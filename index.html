Countries at War, 1823 - 2003


Between 1823 and 2003, 106 countries participated in an inter-state war. This is a visual chronology of the 96 conflicts that involved sustained combat, organized armed forces and that had more than 1,000 battle-related combatant fatalities within a twelve month period. Because of the dataset used, only the states that either omitted 1,000 troops to the war or suffered more than 100 battle related deaths are considered.
