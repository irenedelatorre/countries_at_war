Countries at War 1823-2003


This is a visual chronology of the 96 conflicts that involved sustained combat with organized armed forces and that had more than 1,000 battle-related combatant fatalities between 1823 and 2003.
The dataset used shows the countries that either committed 1,000 troops to the war or suffered more than 100 battle related deaths.
